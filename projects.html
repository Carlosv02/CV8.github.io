A ENGR 102 project to convert a rolling dice to a virtual game using Python. Includes two versions of the game, a menu option, rules of the game, a scorecard, and one or two-player option.

MemAid

The goal of this project is to create and design a toy that addresses the unique needs of the elderly population, with a focus on sensory and memory stimulation, while also being lightweight and portable. This toy should be engaging, safe, and easy to set-up, providing a source of entertainment. Additionally, it should be designed to improve overall well-being by promoting sensory and cognitive function, helping elderly individuals to maintain their mental and physical health as they age.

3lb Combat Robot

This project's current focus is designing and building a 3-pound combat robot to compete in a springshowdown against other colleges. Currently working on designing the drivetrain system and the electrical circuit, while also assembling the chassis and weapon system
